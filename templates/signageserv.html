About Us

Next Gen, Feature Rich Digital Signage at your finger tips

SignageServ is a digital signage product from Adskite, that meets the needs of both enterprise and business users. This feature packed product will enable you in managing the content efficiently by delivering high quality visuals through an easy to use Web and mobile app. Our state-of-the-art technology enables you host 4K & full HD videos. Our SignageServ media player can handle variety of media types like JPEG/PNG/TIFF Image, H.265 Video, PDF, PPT, HTML5 (URLs), Ticker Text etc. You can directly play content from YouTube, Whatsapp, Facebook, Twitter, Mobile etc. SignageServ is used by our customers for different purposes like Entertainment, Informational, Dashboards, Promotional, Visitor engagement, Production Line Management and Other.

Our Products

SignageServ CS Series

You are an organization spread across multiple locations or large number of signages in a single location. You want to manage the content on the signages efficiently.
With the CS Series of SignageServ you gain the advantage of managing your signage content in multiple formats using a central, secure cloud server.
You can create multiple layouts of your content and have complete control on the content, layout and schedule of each signage or a group of signages.

SignageServ ES Series

You are an enterpise with a need to maintain your content within the organizational network.You want to manage your content efficiently and in a targeted manner.
With the ES series of SignageServ you can maintain your content and the signage players with your enterpise LAN. Location specific content can be played using the layout creation and scheduling capabilities.
The solution easily integrates into your corporate security policies.
URL play supports display of Operations dashboards, Production metrics, Sales reports, Defect reports etc.

SignageServ NS Series

You are looking for an easy to use signage app for easy management of content and signages at your business without the need for internet connectivity.
With the NS series of Signageserv, You have a plug-n-play signage manager that lets you create campaigns with multiple formats of content and push them to the signage display.
Instantaneous content like product campaigns, rate card display, welcome messages, voice announcements can be managed at the signage location.
