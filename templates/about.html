About AdsKite

Adskite - Marketplace for outdoor and Indoor advertising. Adskite connects businesses/customers with media/printing vendors for advertising needs.

Are you a business looking for cost efficient advertising?

This Privacy Policy reflects the following three principles that we strive to uphold in providing you with the services on our Services:

  1. Do you know outdoor and indoor advertising is the most effective solution? you can reach 1000s of people everyday.
  2. Do you want to rent/buy the advertising medias from our huge collection of billboards/transit vehicles/bills/tickets/phamplets/newspapers/magazines.
  3. Do you want to find 100s of vendors to advertise at favorite location & with an effective pricing.

Are you a printing/advertising vendor ?

  1. Do you want to advertise your medias/printing services of billboards/transit vehicles/bills/tickets/newspapers/magazines etc.
  2. Do you want to rent/sell your media quickly and earn more money.
  3. Do you want to find 100s of businesses?

In adskite as a business/customer

  1. book/rent/buy billboards.
  2. search/review billboards as per your location & as per pricing needs.
  3. pay an advance amount to book billboards.
  4. communicate with vendors via emails.

In adskite as a vendor

  1. you can register your profile.
  2. add,edit,delete billboards.
  3. rent billboards, accept advance payment.
  4. instant notifications with your customers via emails.
